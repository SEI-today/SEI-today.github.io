Natural Forest Bar 天然氧吧 Ethnic Minorities Land 民族部落 Health Paradise 养生天堂 Cheerful Town 幸福小城

Natural Forest Bar Located in the southwest of yunnan province, Pu 'er has a subtropical monsoon climate.As a result,trees,flowers and all kinds of creatures flourish here.And this provides residents with a great number of natural and delicious ingredients.
There are 25 ethnic minorities in Yunnan, of which 14 are in Pu 'er(哈尼族、彝族、傣族、拉祜族、佤族、布朗族、瑶族等).Every minority has their unique style and culture.Unfortunately, with with the development of urbanizationthese treasure are being lost. How to effectively protect them is a problem that all residents even all mankind should consider.
With its comfortable environment and beautiful starry sky, pu 'er city has been awarded the honorary titles of "China famous city most worthy of being recommended to the world by CCTV", "China's top ten green cities", "China's best leisure city", "China's charming city" and "EMBA most valuable city for investment". Today, these titles have become a resounding "city card" of pu 'er city, passed among citizens, tourists and investors.
Mildness is the biggest characteristic of pu 'er people. People here attach great importance to aesthetics and ceremony, hoping to make life comfortable and happy.Even as more and more young people go abroad to study or work, the gentle and simple character of pu 'er people always remains with them.

《总是慢吞吞的我和南方的蛋黄》

——Liusei

When I was a child, I watched my grandfather did breakfast. I raised my feet just enough to put his waist on his waist. The old man was also very energetic when he was half a year old. He fastened his apron and told me that southerners must pay attention to the sense of ritual.

我还是从小在意别人眼色。
直到等位的情侣发出不耐烦的叹息，才注意到自己吃饭很慢的问题。
小时候看爷爷做早饭，踮起脚手刚够上盘他的腰，老人年过半花甲也很有精神，系紧围裙，告诉我南方人吃饭，一定得注重仪式感。


Cook two eggs, you can't hold them directly.Put pinch of shallots and two drops of sesame oil. After half a bottle of soy sauce darkens the whole piece, usd the bamboo chopsticks crush the egg.
Boiled eggs for 7 minutes, so dense, yolk flowing down like gold. Enve later when I enjoyed salted egg yolk bread at KFC, I was greatfully happy that I didn't miss this delicious.


煮熟两颗鸡蛋，不能直接手握，壳剥光放瓷碟里。
小葱一撮，香油两滴，待半瓶盖酱油使整片色调暗沉后，才赶紧攥起竹筷，戳中间把蛋碾开。
水煮7分钟的蛋，绵绵密密，流心若黄金一样流下来。只要吃惯就不会觉得蛋黄腥了，后来在KFC享受咸蛋黄牛角面包的时候，我也很庆幸没错失此等美味。


When you stay in the north, it's hard to eat flowing eggs.Oue canteen eggs are fully cooked.
When the iron pan was crooked, the light pink ball "ping pong bang" crashed into the stainless steel basin. It's broken in half, but it's convenient for students who rush to class to peel it out in 15 seconds.

Breakfast also has porridge, beef, pies and noodles.But by the time I had almost finished breakfast, the front people had been replaced by three waves

There are no anything special.
It's just that the yolk's sense of flow is never reproduced in my memory.
在北方上学，很少吃得到流心蛋。
食堂鸡蛋都是一筐一筐下水煮，铁锅一歪，淡粉的圆球“乒铃乓啷”砸进不锈钢盆。碎了半边，倒是方便赶课的学生15秒剥出来。

把蛋搁碗里后，我又开始拌小米粥，中间牛肉酥皮咧开，香气若秘境迷雾般弥漫。
就这样坐着，把牛肉馅单独送口，剩下的脆皮来裹温热的粥，等我差不多吃完早餐的时候，前座已经换去三波人了。

同学问我嚼这20分钟有没有什么人生感想。
完全没有。不就是小米的味道、牛肉渍着孜然的味道，豆花绵软得像云朵的味道，五谷芝麻榨出油香的味道。

只是那蛋黄的流心感，再也没有重现在我的记忆里了。

Oh yeah, it's that good. Enjoy in our town.


《黄xixi的豆腐日记》

I thought Bixi was most famous for rice noodles. However,I came here to live and found out that there are also tofu, braised pork and jelly. When you get here, you must remember to order their tofu three to eat——soy milk, sour milk and water tofu.


我以为碧溪最出名的是米线，这趟过来住才发现，这里出名的还有豆腐、卤肉和凉粉。

豆腐摊子最为热闹，一定记得点一点他们的豆腐三吃。哪三吃？现点现做的豆浆、酸浆和水豆腐。 就在庾澄庆家老宅子的隔壁，整条巷子都是坐着吃豆腐的人。




Put pickles and seasonings in the bowl, stir in a spoonful of hot soy milk, stir and stir, let stand for a while, it becomes a state of solidification and not solidification, it is physalis. I personally think that Bixi's physalis is more delicious.
碗里铺上腌菜和佐料，一勺热豆浆打进去，搅拌再搅拌，静置一会儿，变成凝固又不凝固的状态，便是酸浆。
普洱南邦河的村子里有一家豆腐店，也有类似的吃法，但他们家用的是酸笋，豆腐凝固出来比酸菜的要结实，吃起来有些寡。个人觉得，还是碧溪的酸浆好吃一些。



The host said: "If it were not good,why you line up with me."
The water tofu is amazing, smooth and tender. The host was worried that Lao Hu(xixi's hasband) is eating wrongly.
He scooped a spoonful and fed it directly into his mouth: "Do n’t stir the condiments together, remember to eat like this, eh ~"


老板说：“不好吃你上我这儿排队干嘛。”
水豆腐超级棒，又滑又嫩，老板担心老胡吃法不对，舀了一勺直接喂到他嘴里：“不要把佐料搅在一起，记住，要这样吃，诶～”
水豆腐这样好，豆浆当然不必多说。 去之前我们买了卤肉，纯粹是因为顺路。到了发现好几桌都这样，就着卤肉吃豆腐，大约是荤素搭配，味道加倍吧。
